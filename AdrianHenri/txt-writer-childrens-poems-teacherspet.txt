   Teacher’s Pet


   What sort of creature
   is a teacher’s pet?
   What sort of pet
   would a teacher get?

   Would it be a mongoose?
   Would it be a snake?
   Would it be a vampire
   with a thirst to slake?

   Would it be a bulldog
   or a Pekinese?
   Would it be a cuddly kitten
   or a tomcat full of fleas?

   Would it be a blue-eye?
   Would it be a swot?
   What sort of pet
   has your teacher got?
